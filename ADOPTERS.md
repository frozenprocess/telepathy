## How to add your organization

Open a PR that adds a row to the table above. The `Usage` column should briefly
describe how you use Telepathy — for example:

- "NetworkPolicy assertion gate on all policy PRs"
- "Base-vs-PR connectivity diff posted as PR comments"
- "Offline policy inspection during cluster migrations"

No other information is required. Listing your organization here signals to the
community that the project is being used in real environments, which directly
helps the project grow and attract contributors.
